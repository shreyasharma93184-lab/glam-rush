Frequently Asked Questions

Which payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, and Google Pay for online purchases.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping fees and delivery times vary by destination. You can view rates at checkout.
How long will it take to get my order?
Most orders are processed within 1–2 business days (Mon–Fri, excluding holidays) and then shipped via standard delivery (typically 3–5 business days in the USA). Faster express options are available at checkout.
Can I track my order?
Absolutely. Once your order ships, you’ll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. Use this number on the carrier’s website to monitor delivery status.
How do I apply a promo code?
At checkout, enter your discount or promo code in the "Promotional Code" box and click "Apply". The page will update to reflect the discounted total if the code is valid.
Are your products cruelty-free?
Yes, all Glam Rush products are cruelty-free and made with ethically sourced ingredients.

Shipping Information

Processing Time: Orders are typically processed within 1–2 business days (Mon–Fri).

  • Standard Shipping: Free on U.S. orders over $50; otherwise a flat $5.95 fee applies. Delivery usually takes 3–5 business days after shipping.
  • Express Shipping: Optional 2-day and overnight shipping is available for an additional fee (calculated at checkout).
  • International Shipping: Available to select countries; fees and transit times vary by location.

All orders receive a shipping confirmation email with tracking information once they ship. We ship Monday through Friday, excluding holidays.

Return & Refund Policy

If you’re not completely satisfied, you may return unworn, unopened items within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or store credit. Please retain the original packaging and proof of purchase when returning. To initiate a return, contact our customer support or use the online returns portal.

Once we receive your return, refunds are typically processed within 5–7 business days. Please note we cannot refund shipping fees unless the return is due to our error. All returns are subject to review and approval.
